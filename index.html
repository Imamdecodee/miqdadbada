About Me

A journey of passion, patience & persistence

I’m Miqdad Bada A. (Imam_Decoder) — a dreamer, a builder, and a believer in the power of technology to change lives. My love for computers started back in JSS3, when my father took me and my brother to a small computer center. That moment lit a fire in me that has never gone out.

From learning graphics design and printing in secondary school, to late nights of coding and mentorship, my journey has been filled with challenges — but each challenge only made me stronger.

I live by a model I teach my students: the PPTT Model — Patience, Passion, Time, and Tools. With these four, there is no limit to what you can build.

What I Do

I currently serve as the CEO of Ilm Nexus, the developer behind Techies Innovation, and the co-founder of multiple creative ventures. My mission is to use creativity and technology to build solutions that inspire, connect, and drive growth.

I am not just a developer, designer, or entrepreneur. I am a storyteller, a problem-solver, and a leader driven by the belief that with the right mindset, you can turn any idea into impact.
